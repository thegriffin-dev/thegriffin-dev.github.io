About me

Who I am

I'm a System Coordinator with a deep passion for automation and DevOps. My work revolves around making complex systems communicate cleanly, reliably, and without unnecessary human intervention.

I specialize in technical implementations and integrations — designing EDI pipelines, building API connections, and orchestrating tools so they form one coherent operational backbone instead of isolated silos.
